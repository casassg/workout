General Running Tips

Before Your Run

  • • Hydrate well throughout the day
  • • Light meal 2-3 hours before
  • • Dynamic stretching to warm up
  • • Check weather and dress appropriately

After Your Run

  • • Cool down with easy walking
  • • Static stretching for flexibility
  • • Rehydrate and refuel
  • • Note how you felt for next time

Balancing Gym & Running

  • • Don't run hard the day after leg day
  • • Easy runs are great active recovery
  • • Listen to your body - skip if very sore
  • • Prioritize sleep for recovery

Signs to Take It Easy

  • • Persistent fatigue or heavy legs
  • • Elevated resting heart rate
  • • Poor sleep quality
  • • Mood changes or lack of motivation
